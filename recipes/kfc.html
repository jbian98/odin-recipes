Korean Fried Chicken

Description

Korean fried chicken, usually called chikin in Korea, refers to a variety of fried chicken dishes created in South Korea, including the basic huraideu-chicken and spicy yangnyeom chicken.

There are numerous different kinds of recipes you can find on the web, but this is my favourite:

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Buttermilk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • all purpose flour
  • vegetable oil
  • gochugang sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Marinade Chicken in seasonings and buttermilk
  2. Mix Flour and Water to create batter
  3. Dip Chicken in batter
  4. Fry Chicken
  5. Add sauce of choice to Chicken

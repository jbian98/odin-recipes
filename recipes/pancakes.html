Pancakes

Description

Favourite and easiest pancake recipe

Perfect to make on Sunday Morning

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • salt
  • Baking powder
  • Milk and Butter
  • Eggs

Cooking Instructions

  1. Stir all dry ingredients together
  2. Add wet ingredients; stir to combine
  3. Scoop batter onto greased pan
  4. Cook for 2 minutes on each side
  5. Add butter or syrup to serve
